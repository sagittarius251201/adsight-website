Our Full-Stack GAIO Framework

We use a holistic, five-pillar strategy to ensure your brand dominates in the age of AI. Each pillar is essential for building a resilient and authoritative digital presence.

1. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

The technical foundation. We structure your site and content with schema markup and clean code, making it perfectly parsable for AI crawlers.

2. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

Winning the conversational query. We craft content in Q&A formats that directly answers your customers' questions.

3. Foundational SEO

The non-negotiable baseline. Strong traditional SEO is required for AI to find and trust your content in the first place.

4. E-E-A-T Signals

The currency of AI trust. We build your Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trust through verifiable data and expert content.

5. AI Share of Voice (AI SoV)

The new competitive benchmark. We track your citation frequency and sentiment against competitors to constantly refine our strategy.
